The need

If people keep needing something explained, it probably needs a guide.

Maybe it's a product, an app, a workflow, a role, or a process. Maybe you already have videos and notes. Maybe it only exists in someone's head. I help gather the information, organize it, and turn it into something people can follow without needing the same explanation repeated again.

Examples

What can become a guide?

Product or component

A clear reference guide for what it is, how it works, and how people should use it.

App or software walkthrough

A step-by-step tutorial showing users how to navigate the app or complete the important actions.

Workflow or internal process

A practical guide, checklist, or SOP for how the work gets done.

Event role or volunteer instructions

A simple reference people can use to understand where to go, what to do, and how the role works.

Robot, tool, or equipment instructions

A guide for setup, charging, maintenance, operation, or basic troubleshooting.

Customer setup process

A walkthrough customers can follow instead of needing the same explanation manually.

Formats

The final format depends on what people need to do.

Some projects need a PDF-style guide. Some need website-ready content. Some need a short video walkthrough. Some need a checklist or SOP. The format depends on who will use it and what they need to understand.

Process

How it works

  1. 01

    Show me what needs explaining

    You can send videos, notes, screenshots, old docs, or just explain the situation.

  2. 02

    I gather context

    I ask questions, review the material, and figure out what people actually need to understand.

  3. 03

  4. 04
