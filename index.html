The need

If people keep needing something explained, it probably needs a guide.

Maybe it's a product, an app, a workflow, a role, or a process. Maybe you already have videos and notes. Maybe it only exists in someone's head. I help gather the information, organize it, and turn it into something people can follow without needing the same explanation repeated again.

Case metadata

What needed explaining

  • A robotics component called Morpheus Drive
  • How the component worked
  • How it connected to the app
  • How the app controlled it over Bluetooth

Process

How it works

  1. 01

    Show me what needs explaining

    You can send videos, notes, screenshots, old docs, or just explain the situation.

  2. 02

    I gather context

    I ask questions, review the material, and figure out what people actually need to understand.

  3. 03

    I organize the explanation

    I turn the information into a clear structure that makes sense to the person using it.

  4. 04

    I make the guide

    I create the walkthrough, reference doc, checklist, SOP, tutorial, or training material.

  5. 05

    You review it

    You check for accuracy and tell me what needs adjusting.

  6. 06

    I polish the final version

    You get a clear guide people can actually use.
