Process

How a project runs.

Every project moves through these five steps. Scope and timeline are agreed upfront so you know what you're getting before any work begins.

  1. 01

    Discovery & fit check

    You tell me what already exists, what's not working today, and who the content is for. We confirm it's a fit before either of us commits time. Free.

    • 30-min call or written brief
    • Honest yes/no on fit
  2. 02

    Knowledge intake

    You send me what you have. Docs, recordings, screenshots, half-finished drafts, or a 30-minute call walking through the process out loud. I work from raw material so you're not also writing.

    • Shared folder for materials
    • One or two clarifying calls if needed
  3. 03

    Structuring & build

    I organize the material into a clear structure, rewrite it for the audience you named, and format it into the agreed deliverable. You don't hear from me daily, you hear from me with a draft.

    • Outline shared before drafting (for larger projects)
    • Most small projects delivered in 1–2 weeks
  4. 04

    Review & revision

    You review the first draft and send notes. One round of revision is included in every project. Anything beyond that gets scoped before I start it, so there are no surprise charges.

    • 1 revision round included
    • Additional rounds priced upfront
  5. 05

    Final delivery

    You get the final deliverable plus every source file behind it. Drop it into your LMS, knowledge base, or wherever it needs to live. You own all of it.

    • Final exports (PDF, video, slides, docs)
    • All source files included
